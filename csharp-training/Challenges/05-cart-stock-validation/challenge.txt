What?
Our stores sell products with limited stock. Build a small shopping cart that can add products with validation.

Required behavior
Implement AddToCart(int productId, int quantity) that:
 1. Adds the product to the cart (or increases quantity if already in cart)
 2. Validates before changing the cart
 
Validation Rules
+---------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------+
| Rule                                                          | If violated                 |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------+
| quantity must be greater than 0                               | Reject with a clear message |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------+
| Product must exist in the catalog                             | Reject                      |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------+
| Total quantity in cart (existing + new) must not exceed       | Reject                      |
| available stock                                               |                             |
+---------------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------+

On success: update the cart and return normally.
On failure: do not modify the cart — throw an exception or return a result object (your choice; be ready to explain why).

Also implement GetCartTotal() — sum of quantity × unitPrice for all items in the cart.
